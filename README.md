# 🌿 Visual Identity: Premium Organic Food Delivery

## 👤 About Me
Hi! I am a passionate UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer. Currently, I am expanding my industry skillset as a UI/UX Design Intern at **DecodeLabs**. This project is a part of my industrial training, focusing on creating clean, user-centered digital experiences that balance beautiful design with absolute functional utility.

---

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on designing a premium, clean, and trusted interface for a modern organic food delivery service. 

### Key Screens Designed:
1. **Splash Screen:** The first screen a user sees, designed to make a strong brand impression.
2. **Product List Screen:** A clean grid layout where users can easily browse different organic products.
3. **Product Details Screen:** A detailed page showing specific product information, pricing, and trust badges.

---

## 🎨 Design Elements

* **Color Palette:** Used fresh Leaf Green for health, Earthy Brown for a natural connection, and premium Gold accents. High-stress fast-food reds were avoided.
* **Typography:** Used elegant Serif fonts for headings to give a premium feel, and clean Sans-Serif fonts for easy reading of prices and descriptions.
* **Realism:** The design uses natural, soft lighting for food images to keep the look authentic and fresh.
